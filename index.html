JOURNEY SO FAR



Hairdresser Salon was founded in 2017 with a vision to redefine salon services and provide a luxurious grooming experience. The brand is led by Tushar Srivastava, Creative Director and Owner, whose expertise and passion for hairstyling continue to set new standards in the industry. With numerous accolades under his belt, Tushar combines artistry with precision to deliver world-class styling solutions. Hairdresser Salon is committed to ensuring every client walks out with confidence and a smile.

Tushar Shrivastava

Tushar Srivastava, Creative Director and Owner of Hairdresser Salon, is a visionary artist admired by hairstylists for his exceptional technical expertise and creative flair. His illustrious journey includes earning prestigious qualifications such as the Absolute Route to Hairdressing Beginner's Diploma at Arth L'Oréal Professional, the Technical Master (Passport Class) from L'Oréal ACADEMY, And Masters in Cut and Color by renowned Malaysian Hairstylist Sumon Koh at the L'Oréal Technical Centre, and Tushar’s dedication and skill have elevated Hairdresser to a beacon of innovation and excellence in the salon industry.
